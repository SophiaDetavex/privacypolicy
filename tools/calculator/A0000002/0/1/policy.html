FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 characters max)
Great Calculator is the powerful, all-in-one calculator designed for
students, professionals, and anyone who needs reliable math tools. With
three distinct modes and smart display features, it's the only calculator
app you'll ever need.
THREE POWERFUL MODES
Basic Calculator
Perfect for everyday math. Clean, intuitive design with large buttons for
quick calculations. Includes percentage, memory functions, and
parentheses for complex expressions.
Scientific Calculator
Full-featured scientific calculator with trigonometric functions (sin,
cos, tan), logarithms (log, ln), powers, roots, and constants (, e).
Perfect for algebra, trigonometry, and calculus homework.
Graphing Calculator
Plot mathematical functions with an interactive graph. Trace along curves
to find exact coordinates. Essential for visualizing equations and
understanding mathematical relationships.
SMART DISPLAY MODES
Fractions Mode - See answers as proper fractions ( instead of
0.333...)
Radicals Mode - Display square roots with symbols (2 instead
of 1.414...)
Percent Mode - Keep percentage symbols in your results
ALGEBRA & EQUATION SOLVING
Enter equations with variables using the ABC keyboard. Great Calculator
solves linear, quadratic, cubic, quartic, and quintic equations - up to
degree 5! Perfect for checking homework and learning algebra.
BUILT FOR STUDENTS
Whether you're in middle school learning fractions or in college tackling
calculus, Great Calculator grows with you. The fraction and radical
display modes make it perfect for showing your work the way teachers
expect.
PREMIUM FEATURES INCLUDED

Memory functions (MC, MR, M+)
Haptic feedback for satisfying button presses
Works offline - no internet required
Privacy focused - no data collection
