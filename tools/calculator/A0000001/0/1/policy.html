Welcome to our Calculator App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure.

2. Information We Collect

Our Calculator App is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal to no personal information:

2.1 Information You Provide

The Calculator App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use its core functionality. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited technical information to improve app performance, including device type, operating system version, and app version. This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

If the app includes a calculation history feature, this data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You may clear this history at any time through the app settings.

Any information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Calculator App experience, ensure app stability and performance, fix bugs and technical issues, and develop new features based on usage patterns.

If our app displays advertisements, we may work with advertising partners who may collect certain information about your device and app usage to deliver relevant ads. You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Calculator App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.
